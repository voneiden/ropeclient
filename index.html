  • Colors

How to use

Configure your config.txt

To talk as your character, simply type text. Typing hello! becomes Rogar says, "Hello!"

To roll a dice, type !XdY anywhere in your text. !dY is fine too.

To do an action (/me), type *grins.. The output will be Rogar grins.

To do a freeform description, type
#The sky turns darker as the day nears its end.. Suddenly, Rogar notices something moving by the cliffs!
Output will again look like
(Gamemaster) The sky turns darker as the day nears its end.. Suddenly, Rogar notices something moving by the cliffs!

Various coloring commands are also available:

  • /re (turns text color to red)
  • /bl (turns text color to blue)
  • /gr (turns text color to green)
  • /ye (turns text color to yellow)
  • /cl (clear - resets text color to previous color set)
